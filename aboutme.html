About Me

Hey there!

My name is Megan! I'm a former Video Producer/Editor turned Software Engineer! My belief about life is that everything has a story. Even coding!

I enjoy using my expertise from the creative field in my coding. Sometimes, you need to figure out a new creative way to work around a project, whether that's in editing a video or rewriting code.

I'm a proud graduate from the University of North Texas. After graduation, I worked on both freelance and full time video projects with different companies including Kwik Trip and AT&T.

Outside of work, I can usually be found writing short stories for fun (would love to be published one day!), baking, spending time with my family and friend, and working on puzzles. I'm a proud dog mom of a corgi named Ophelia (Ophie for short) and cat mom of a cat named Lolo.
